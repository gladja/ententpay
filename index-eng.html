FAQ

Answers to popular questions.

  • What is EntentPay?

    EntentPay is a global payment platform that enables businesses to accept online payments via websites, mobile apps, social media, and even offline — all managed through a unified system.

  • What payment methods do you support?

    We support bank cards (Visa, Mastercard, UnionPay, and others), digital wallets, as well as bank transfers, mobile payments, and local e-wallets — all familiar to your customers.

  • How much does it cost to connect?

    Connecting to the EntentPay payment service is free.

  • How often are payouts made?

    Payouts follow a custom schedule — you choose what works best. Everything is lexible and negotiable.

  • How can I track payment information?

    All data is available in your dashboard: transaction details, reports, history, API notifications, and real-time balance.

  • Why choose EntentPay?

    Because with us, it’s simple: quick setup, flexible payout system, fraud protection, user-friendly interface, and real human support.
